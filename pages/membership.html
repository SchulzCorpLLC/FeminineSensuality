Are the virtual circles recorded?

Yes, to honor all timezones and schedules, our live sisterhood circles and workshops are recorded and added to our members-only library within 24 hours for you to watch at your convenience.

The Circle is a sanctuary for all who identify as women. We are committed to creating a diverse, inclusive, and brave space where every voice is honored and every story is sacred. We welcome you exactly as you are.
