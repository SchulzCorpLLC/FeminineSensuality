Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Inner Circle for?

The Inner Circle is for high-achieving women who feel a deep calling to reconnect with their feminine essence. If you've found success in the material world but feel disconnected from your body, your sensuality, and your inner wisdom, this is your sanctuary.

What if I can't make the live calls?

No problem at all. Every single live session, workshop, and wisdom circle is recorded and uploaded to our private resource library within 24 hours. You can access the replays at any time that suits your schedule.

What is your cancellation policy?

For monthly memberships, you can cancel at any time, and your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. For annual and lifetime plans, these are commitments to a deeper journey and are non-refundable after 14 days.
